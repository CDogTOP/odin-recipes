Tacos

Description

Here, right there, we have TOP's out-of-this-world tacos! Beautifully made with ground beef, tortillas, tomatoes, corn, and cheese, you're sure to love these tacos! Don't give up a spectacular opportunity today, try our taco recipe out with family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tortillas
  • Oil
  • Sauces of your choice (ketchup, mayonnaise, etc)
  • Spices (pepper, garlic powder, cumin, chili powder)
  • Toppings and garnishes of your choice (tomatoes, corn, cilantro, etc)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef thoroughly and however you'd like
  2. Prepare your tortillas (fry them lightly in oil)
  3. Fill the taco shells with the ground beef
  4. Apply toppings and garnishes over the beef
  5. Slide them out onto a plate and serve!
