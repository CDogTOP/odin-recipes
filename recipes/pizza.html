Pizza

Description

This is TOP's finest dish straight from Italy! Feast your eyes on our one-of-a-kind pepperoni pizza! Topped and loaded with melted cheese and fresh pepperoni, this recipe is sure to give you and your friends and family a tastastic night! What're you waiting for, go on and try it yourself today!

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-purchased will do just fine!)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Any seasonings or garnishes of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes
  2. Prepare your pizza sauce (whether store-purchased or homemade, prep the sauce so that it suits the size of your pizza best!)
  3. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle and leave it to rest for 5-6 minutes
  4. Apply the sauce and toppings to the pizza
  5. Cook the pizza in the oven for 12-15 minutes for a perfect crust!
  6. Slice the pizza up and serve!
